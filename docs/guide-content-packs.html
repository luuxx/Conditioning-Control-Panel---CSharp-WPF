Overview

Content packs are curated collections of images and videos for use with the conditioning engine. They provide a quick way to get started with high-quality content without having to source and organize your own files.

Downloading

  1. Navigate to the Content Packs section in the app (Patreon tab)
  2. Click a pack to begin downloading
  3. Wait for the download to complete — progress is shown in the UI

Downloads support resume — if interrupted, they pick up where they left off. This is achieved using HTTP Range headers for efficient partial downloads.

  • Up to 10 automatic retry attempts with exponential backoff
  • Download timeout: 30 minutes per pack

Managing Packs

  • Packs are stored in a hidden .packs/ folder inside your assets directory
  • Files are encrypted with AES encryption and renamed with GUIDs
  • An encrypted manifest tracks the contents of each pack
  • Pack images are automatically available in the flash rotation once installed
  • Pack videos appear in the mandatory video pool
  • To remove a pack, use the in-app management interface
üìù Note

Don't manually modify files in the .packs/ folder — the encryption and manifest system expects specific file names. Use the in-app tools instead.

Bandwidth & Limits

Downloads are rate-limited to prevent abuse. Patreon subscribers receive higher bandwidth limits.

The server tracks:

  • Downloads remaining (daily limit)
  • Bandwidth used vs. bandwidth limit
  • Reset time for limits

If you hit the rate limit, you'll receive an error with the reset time. Successful downloads are reported to the server; failed downloads refund bandwidth.

Limit Type Details
Daily Downloads Limited per day, resets at midnight UTC
Bandwidth Total GB tracked per period
Patreon Bonus Higher limits for active subscribers
Rate Limit HTTP 429 returned when exceeded

Troubleshooting

Download stuck or failing

  • Check your internet connection
  • The app will retry up to 10 times automatically
  • Close and reopen the app — downloads resume from where they left off

"Rate limit exceeded" error

  • You've hit the daily download limit
  • Wait until the displayed reset time
  • Patreon subscribers have higher limits

Pack not showing in rotation

  • Ensure the download completed fully (check progress)
  • Restart the app after download completes
  • Check that the pack isn't disabled in pack management

"Offline mode" blocking downloads

  • Disable Offline Mode in Settings to allow downloads
  • Downloads require an active internet connection
