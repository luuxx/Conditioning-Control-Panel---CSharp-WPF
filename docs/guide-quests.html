Daily & Weekly Quests

Complete daily and weekly challenges for bonus XP and maintain your streak

Overview

Quests are challenges that reward bonus XP for completing specific activities. They give you daily and weekly goals to work toward, keeping your conditioning routine fresh and rewarding.

  • 3 daily quests and 3 weekly quests are active at any time
  • Quest XP scales by +2% per level, so higher-level users earn more from the same quests
  • Quests are part of the seasonal system ‚Äî progress resets each season

Quest Refresh

  • Daily quests refresh every 24 hours
  • Weekly quests refresh once per week
  • Quest definitions are fetched from the server and cached for 24 hours
  • If offline, quests use the last cached definitions

Rerolls

Rerolls are NOT available by default ‚Äî they require Skill Tree unlocks:

  • Quest Refresh skill: 1 free daily reroll
  • Reroll Addict skill: +2 additional daily rerolls (3 total)
  • Better Quests skill: +25% XP on rerolled quests
üí° Tip

Invest in the Skill Tree's quest branch to maximize your daily XP gains. See the Skill Tree guide for details.

Streaks

Complete at least 1 daily quest per day to maintain your streak. Streaks reward consistency with increasing bonuses.

Streak Multiplier

With the "Streak Power" skill, earn +0.5% XP per streak day (max +15% at 30 days).

Milestone Rewards

The "Milestone Rewards" skill gives a daily XP bonus based on your streak tier:

Streak Days Daily Bonus
1-3 days 50 XP
4-6 days 100 XP
7-13 days 150 XP
14-29 days 200 XP
30+ days 300 XP

Streak Protection

  • "Good Girl Streak" skill: 1 free streak shield per week (forgives a missed day)
  • "Oopsie Insurance" skill: recover a broken streak for 500 XP (once per season)
⚠️ Warning

Your streak resets if you miss a day without a shield! The Oopsie Insurance skill can save you once per season.

Seasonal Quests

Quests follow the game's seasonal system:

  • Seasons run on a monthly basis (format: YYYY-MM)
  • Each season has a fun themed title
  • Some quests may only be available during specific date ranges
  • Quest progress resets at the start of each season

Check the Leaderboard & Seasons guide for more about how seasons work.
