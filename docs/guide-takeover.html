Overview

Takeover Mode (also called Autonomy Mode) allows your companion to act independently, triggering flashes, videos, subliminals, and other effects on her own schedule. She becomes an active participant in your conditioning rather than just responding to your commands.

How It Works

Trigger Types

Trigger Description
Random Interval Actions trigger at random intervals based on your settings (e.g., every 5-15 minutes)
Idle Detection When you've been inactive, the companion may try to get your attention
Context Awareness With Window Awareness enabled, triggers based on what you're doing

Action Flow

  1. Timer fires based on settings
  2. System checks if an action can be taken (not on cooldown, no active interaction)
  3. Companion selects an action based on weights and mood
  4. Optionally announces the action with a teasing message
  5. Executes the action (flash, video, etc.)
  6. Enters cooldown period before next action

Available Actions

Each action can be individually enabled or disabled:

Mood System

The companion's mood changes based on time of day, affecting which actions are more likely:

Time Mood Behavior
6 AM - 12 PM Gentle Prefers comments, reduces disruptive actions
12 PM - 6 PM Attentive Balanced behavior
6 PM - 10 PM Playful More bubbles and flashes
10 PM - 6 AM Mischievous More videos, brain drain, and subliminals
üí° Time-Aware Mode

Enable "Time-Aware Intensity" in settings to let the mood system also affect action frequency, not just selection.

Settings

Core Settings

Setting Description

Idle Timeout How long before idle triggers activate
Cooldown Minimum time between actions
Intensity Higher intensity = more disruptive actions become more likely
Announcement Chance Probability of announcing actions before executing
