Connecting Your Account

  1. Go to the Patreon tab in the app
  2. Click Login with Patreon
  3. Authorize the app in your browser
  4. Your patron status will be verified automatically

Patreon Status

View your connection status in the Patreon tab:

  • Connected - Shows your patron name
  • Tier - Shows your support level
  • AI Status - Shows remaining daily requests

AI Chat

Chat directly with Bambi through the avatar for personalized interactions.

How to Use

  • Double-click the avatar to open the chat input
  • Type your message and press Enter
  • Receive personalized AI responses in speech bubbles

Features

  • Personality - Bambi adapts to your interactions over time

Window Awareness

The avatar reacts to what you're doing on your computer, providing contextual commentary and encouragement.

What It Detects

  • Active window names
  • Browser tab titles
  • Time spent on activities
  • Shopping, gaming, social media, and more

Settings

Setting Description
Enable Awareness Toggle activity detection
Reaction Cooldown Minimum seconds between reactions (30-600s)
